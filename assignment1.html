Assignment 1: Create a Name Plate.





UX Development Process

Research Question: Who is the user of the Name Plate?

  • NOT myself, I already know who I am
  • NOT friends or family, they know who I am
  • Observation of Jami's environment: Fellow workers who have heard of Jami but do not know what I look like or where I sit.target user

Reserch Question: What context will the name plate be used?

  • NOT in my home, I don't need to find myself
  • Observation: Likely in my place of work

Problem Statement

  • Co-workers need a way to find Jami Odell in a large business complex because they want to give him information or ask him a question in person.

"How Might We Statement"

  • HMW help fellow co-workers easily and quickly locate Jami Odell so they can ask him a question or give him information?

Hypothosis

  • We believe that a Name Plate will help fellow co-workers quickly and easily locate Jami inside of a large office.

UX Research Methods

  • Conducted contextual discovery interviews with fellow co-workers: Why do you seek out co-workers in person?

Findings

When searching for Jami Odell inside of the corporate office, users:
  • Need to know where Jami's desk is
  • Are very busy with limited time
  • May know Jami by name and skillset, but not by appearance
  • Search for Jami starting in the general vacinity
  • Scan the isles for name plates
If not at desk, users need to:
  • Know when Jami will be back
  • Leave Jami a Message
  • Have a way to contact Jami in the future
  • Easily remember where he sits
Competitive analysis:
  • Most name plates focus on aesthetics, not usability
  • fall into 3 styles: Aluminum, wood, plexiglass

Design approach

Create and artifact that meets user needs that includes the following features, in order of value to user:
  • Easy to spot,legible name so users can quickly locate Jami's desk
  • Message system so users can leave Jami a message if he is
  • Return time system so users know when Jami will return
  • Business Card so users know how more about what Jami does and how to contact him

Usability Teseting

  • Would not use post it notes system, just send email
  • Thought the image should be larger
  • Said they would not take a business card
  • Need to see name from end of the row
