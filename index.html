Assignment 1







The assigment was to create a name plate

UW Research Questions

Who is the users of the name plate?

  • NOT myself, I already know who I am
  • NOT friends or family, they know who I am
  • Observation: Likely fellow workers who have heard of me but do not know me personally

What context will the name plate be used?

  1. NOT in my home, I don't need to find myself
  2. Observation: Likely in my place of work, where it is common for co-workers to know the name of a co-worker but do not know where they sit in the building or what they look like

What is the How Might We statement?

  1. HMW help fellow co-workers easily and quickly locate Jami Odell so they can ask him a question of give him information?

What is the problem statement

  1. Co-workers need a way to find Jami Odell in a large business complex because they want to give him information or ask him a question in person.

What is the hypothosis

  1. We believe that a Name Plate will help fellow co-workers quickly and easily locate me inside of a large office building so they can provide me with important information or ask me a question.

Research

  1. Conducted contextual interviews with fellow co-workers to discover what they are intending to do when seeking me out for person to person interactions.

Findings

When searching for Jami Odell inside of the corporate office, users:
  1. Only Jami Odell only by name and skillset, but not by appearance
  2. Search for Jami by navigating to the general vacinity
  3. Quickly walk up and down isles looking for the desk of Jami Odell or his name plate
  4. Want to deliver important information in person
  5. Want to ask Jami a question in Person in person
If not at desk, users:
  1. Know when Jami will be back
  2. Leave Jami a Message
  3. Know a bit more about what Jami does
  4. Have a way to contact Jami in the future
  5. Easily remember where he sits

Design approach

Create and artifact that meets user needs that includes the following features, in order of value to user:
  1. Only Jami Odell only by name and skillset, but not by appearance
  2. Search for Jami by navigating to the general vacinity
  3. Quickly walk up and down isles looking for the desk of Jami Odell or his name plate
  4. Want to deliver important information in person
  5. Want to ask Jami a question in Person in person
